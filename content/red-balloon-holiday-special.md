UPDATE (January 1, 2020): This offer has ended! Thank you everybody who participated, payments have been sent out. We will be announcing a similar offer shortly for January and beyond.

Need some extra cash for the holiday season? RezScore has a great deal for you! For a limited time, we’re providing $10 per referral in our Red Balloon program, whether or not they get the job.

Typically, bounties only pay out when the companies finalize the hire, but this can be slow going. We’re going to jump the gun this December and payout $10 per referral, whether or not your referrals get hired.

Of course, we reserve the right to withhold any payouts if we suspect spam, fraud, or any reason at our discretion. But we are quite serious about wanting to give back to the community and grow our Red Balloon program for 2020.

To get started, our Red Balloon dashboard is available at https://jobs.rezscore.com/redballoon. Once you are registered, you can copy a link for any job. These links contain a token that gives you credit whenever anybody applies through your link. Send your friends and track your clicks, applications, and earnings!
