RezScore is a [free resume grader](https://ai.rezscore.com/), data-driven from the ground up. Upload your resume and in seconds
you get two honest grades, a map of where you stand, and a clear path to a
stronger version.

The video above shows the whole flow. Here is what you are watching:

1. **Upload and see the card fill in.** Drop in your resume and the report
   card appears almost instantly: tabs, a chart, and your first grade land
   before the deeper read is even done. The rest fills in live, a few seconds
   behind it, so you are never staring at a blank spinner.
2. **Two grades, not one.** Presentation is how persuasively your resume
   communicates what you bring. Qualifications is how strong a candidate you
   are for the role you are targeting, judged from the substance on the page,
   the skills, the experience, the trajectory, independent of how well the
   document dresses it up. A beautifully written page over thin substance and
   a strong candidate whose resume undersells them can land the same letter
   grade on the old single score. Now you see both, and they usually do not
   match.
3. **See where you land on the map.** Your two grades plot as a dot on a
   quadrant chart: Qualifications across, Presentation up. Apply to jobs,
   Polish your resume, Build your skills, or Get started, the quadrant names
   the honest next move instead of leaving you to guess.
4. **Check the target and salary, correct with one tap.** We infer the role
   you are aiming for from the resume itself and show our guess with an edit
   button right next to it. Guessed wrong? One tap fixes the whole read. The
   salary row works the same way: an estimate you can slide to tell us where
   you are actually aiming.
5. **Talk to Jen.** Ask our AI career advisor anything about your resume. She
   answers like a sharp peer with specific, do-this-next suggestions, not vague
   filler, and she knows which of your two grades needs the work.

We built RezScore because most resume advice is generic and most resume tools are
black boxes. Ours is the opposite: every grade is explainable, every suggestion
is specific, and the whole first pass is free. Over 13 million resumes have been
analyzed.
