RezScore is a free, data-driven resume grader. Upload your resume and in seconds
you get an honest A to F grade, a breakdown of what is working and what is not,
and a clear path to a stronger version.

The 60-second video above shows the whole flow. Here is what you are watching:

1. **Upload and grade.** Drop in your resume and get an instant grade with a
   category breakdown (evidence and impact, positioning, structure,
   parseability and ATS, credibility), plus your biggest strength and biggest
   weakness.
2. **Talk to Jen.** Ask our AI career advisor anything about your resume. She
   answers like a sharp peer with specific, do-this-next suggestions, not vague
   filler.
3. **See your salary range.** Get an estimate of the target salary your resume
   supports, and adjust it to tell us where you are aiming.
4. **Target a job.** Name a role or paste a job posting and watch your resume
   rebuild itself to fit it, new headline, sharper summary, reordered bullets.
   Your first targeted resume is free.
5. **Explore your skills.** See how your skills map against real market demand in
   the Skills Explorer.

We built RezScore because most resume advice is generic and most resume tools are
black boxes. Ours is the opposite: every grade is explainable, every suggestion
is specific, and the whole first pass is free. Over 13 million resumes have been
analyzed.

This page is our standing walkthrough. We update the video as the product
improves, so what you see above is always the current RezScore.
